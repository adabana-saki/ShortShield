ShortShield - Block Short-Form Videos

Take back your time! ShortShield blocks short-form videos from popular platforms, helping you stay focused and productive.

FEATURES

- Block YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels
- One-click toggle to enable/disable blocking
- Whitelist specific channels or accounts you want to allow
- View statistics on blocked content
- No data collection - your privacy is respected

WHY SHORTSHIELD?

Short-form videos are designed to keep you scrolling endlessly. ShortShield helps you:

- Focus on what matters
- Reduce mindless scrolling
- Reclaim hours of your day

PRIVACY FOCUSED

- No data collection
- No external servers
- No tracking
- 100% open source

LANGUAGES

English, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Korean, Spanish, Portuguese, German, French

OPEN SOURCE

ShortShield is open source! Check the code, report issues, or contribute:
https://github.com/adalab/shortshield

PERMISSIONS

- Storage: Save your settings locally
- Tabs: Detect which website you're on
- Host permissions: Run on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram
